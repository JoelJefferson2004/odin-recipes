Biriyani

Royal Euphoria: Crafting a Biryani Masterpiece

Elevate your senses with our Michelin-inspired Biryani—a regal fusion of fragrant Basmati rice, marinated meats, and artisanal spices, crowned with caramelized onions. A symphony of flavors fit for royalty.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken, Lamb, or Vegetables
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Marinate meat with yogurt, spices, and herbs for 2 hours.
  2. Caramelize onions; set half aside.
  3. Sauté spices, ginger, garlic, and tomatoes in ghee.
  4. Layer parboiled rice with saffron, onions, and herbs.
  5. Arrange marinated meat, cashews, raisins, more rice, and herbs.
  6. Slow-cook sealed pot for rich flavors.
  7. Fluff layers; garnish with almonds and onions.
  8. Serve with minty yogurt raita.
  9. Indulge in a symphony of taste and aroma.
