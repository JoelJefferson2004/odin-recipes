The Dosa

the best thing you can ask for breakfast!

Michelin-starred enchantment meets South Indian tradition: A meticulously crafted dosa masterpiece adorned with citrus-infused elegance, accompanied by a trio of artisanal chutneys that elevate flavors to celestial realms.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Urad Dal
  • Salt and water

Instructions

  1. Heat the pan for 2 minutes and check whether the pan is hot enough by sprinkling water onto the pan
  2. Once the ozzze sound is heard pour a whole scoop of dosa batter onto the tawa and spread evenly.
  3. Wait for a minutes until it becomes brown and then flip it over.
  4. Flip it over once again when you feel like doing it.
  5. Tada you have.
